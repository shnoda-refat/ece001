Advantages:

  • Reducing error, artificial intelligence helps us reduce error and the opportunity to reach accuracy with greater accuracy
  • Exploring the difficult as using these complex machines to explore the bottom ocean and thus overcoming the limits of mankind
  • Digital Assistants: Highly advanced organizations use ‘avatars’ which are replicas or digital assistants who can actually interact with the users, thus saving the need for human resources
  • Medical applications, doctors evaluate patients with "AI" help, inform them of the side effects of different medications. Radiosurgery is a common application of artificial intelligence
  • No breaks, machines, unlike humans, do not require frequent breaks and refreshments. They are programmed for long hours and can perform continuously without getting bored, distracted or even tired
